About

I build computer systems that support scientific research, automate stuff, process data or visualize information. I do that by composing simple elements into robust systems, by talking to people trying to find a ubiquitous language, by looking for abstractions and patterns.

Proud husband of Ula and father of Simona. Trying to find my place in Białystok, Poland. I search for rhythms and patterns around me, in nature, music and art. In my free time I create music, grow plants, fire ceramics, make pictures of local street art and read about science stuff with focus on physics, astronomy, biology and maths.
